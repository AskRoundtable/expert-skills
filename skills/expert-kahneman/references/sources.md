## Source Note

This module was built from Claude's training knowledge of Kahneman's published work. No primary EPUB was available locally. Key frameworks are drawn from:

1. **Thinking, Fast and Slow** (primary) — the main source for all 10 mental models, 7 patterns, cue sensitivity, and blind spots
2. **Academic papers** — Kahneman & Tversky (1974, 1979) provide the original formulations of heuristics/biases and prospect theory
3. **Noise** (secondary) — provides the replication critique discussion in blind-spots.md

**Confidence by topic:**
- System 1/2, WYSIATI, Planning Fallacy, Inside/Outside View: 0.95 (central theses of TFAS, extensively documented)
- Prospect Theory, Loss Aversion: 0.95 (Nobel-winning work, replicated extensively)
- Anchoring, Availability, Representativeness: 0.93 (core heuristics, well-replicated)
- Cognitive Ease, Peak-End Rule: 0.88 (from TFAS, some peripheral effects have weaker replication)
- Focusing Illusion: 0.90 (well-established finding)
